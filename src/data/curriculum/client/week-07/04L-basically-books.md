By the end of this lab, you should have a collection in Postman that has the following endpoints:

- **GET (READ)**
  - All Books
  - All Authors
  - Books on Sale
  - Favorite Authors
  - Books by UID
  - Authors by UID
  - Author Books
  - Single Book
  - Single Author
- **POST (CREATE - needs payload)**
  - Book
  - Author
- **PATCH (UPDATE - needs payload)**
  - Book
  - Author
- **DELETE**
  - Book
  - Author
